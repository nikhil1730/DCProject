About This WebSite

We are here to save the shopping time for the customers' and to help with increase the profit in business for Business Owners. Our website provides the Wait Time feature for the multiple stores with different locations. Business owners are required to pay the subscription charges to utilize our service. If business owners have registered or subscribed our service, then customers can find those stores informations (i.e. business owner's name, store name, store location, the number of people inside that store, the number of people in the queue and the total wait time) on our Dashboard without login or paying any subscription charges.
